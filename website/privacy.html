Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 10, 2025

Introduction

MindToss ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

MindToss is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Email Address: The email address you provide is stored locally on your device only. We do not transmit or store your email on our servers.
  • Notes and Content: Your text notes, voice memos, and photos are stored locally on your device and sent directly to your email. We never access, store, or process this content.
  • App Preferences: Settings like dark mode preference are stored locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Send your captured thoughts to your specified email address
  • Remember your preferences within the app
  • Provide app functionality

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms. We do not operate servers that store your personal data. Your notes are sent directly from your device to your email provider.

Third-Party Services

MindToss uses your device's native mail client to send emails. We do not share your data with any third parties for marketing or analytics purposes.

Camera and Microphone

MindToss requests access to:

  • Camera: To capture photos of your thoughts and ideas
  • Microphone: To record voice memos
  • Photo Library: To attach existing images to your notes

These permissions are used solely for capturing content that you choose to send to yourself. Media is never uploaded to our servers.

Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your data. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

MindToss is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
