Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add an email address?

Tap the Settings icon in the top-left corner, then tap "Add Email Account" to enter your email address. This is where your notes will be sent.

Why can't I send emails?

MindToss uses your device's built-in mail app to send emails. Make sure you have the Mail app configured with an email account, or check that you've granted mail permissions.

How do I record a voice memo?

Tap the "Voice" tab at the top, then tap the microphone button to start recording. Tap the stop button when you're done, then tap "TOSS" to send it to your email.

Where are my notes stored?

Your notes are sent directly to your email inbox. MindToss also keeps a local history on your device, which you can view by tapping the clock icon in the top-right corner.

How do I enable dark mode?

Go to Settings (gear icon) and toggle the "Dark Mode" switch.

Can I use multiple email addresses?

Yes! In Settings, you can add multiple email accounts. Tap on the email selector at the top of the main screen to switch between them.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone to manage or cancel your MindToss subscription.

How do I delete my data?

All your data is stored locally on your device. To delete all data, simply delete the MindToss app. Your sent emails will remain in your email inbox.
